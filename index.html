About me

I am half-French, half-Dutch and have been living in London for 12 years. I am a consultant and sales person with a MBA and MSc in Computer Science and experience in industries like luxury goods, healthcare and finance.

I came to realise that in all my positions, I enjoyed myself most when I was programming and building new tools to solve business challenges and improve efficiency. I therefore decided to go back to what I like most, coding. I really enjoy the craftsmanship, creativity and problem solving that come with it, as well as the continuous learning process.

GA gave me the opportunity to be part of their web development immersive course and I haven’t looked back. I am looking forward to joining an innovative company with strong work ethics, that promotes diversity and puts a strong emphasis on developing its employees.

Interests

For as long I can remember I have been passionate about wildlife and nature. As a result, many of my hobbies revolve around animals and animal welfare. I am a keen wildlife photographer, I love to read my National Geographic back to back or going to the Natural History Museum.

I absolutely loved being a farmyard volunteer at the Spitalfield City Farm which involved going to the farm every Sunday morning at 9am to clean the stables and feed the animals. My next adventure would be to travel abroad for a voluntary conservation mission.

I am also a very keen skier having spend numerous holidays in the French Alps enjoying the slopes and local dishes.

I am big fan of travelling, languages and interculturality having been raised bilingual and having travelled/studies/worked abroad on many occasions.

Last but not least, I enjoy old fashioned games (Mario and Zelda are high on the list), Italian cuisine, French literature, miyazaki cartoons and watching random Netflix series.
